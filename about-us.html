History

Chicago is one of the most diverse, creative, and innovative cities in the United States. Located in the heart of the Midwest, it is a connector between the nation’s two coasts. It is also ranked among the top five most segregated cities in America. Despite the innovation, opportunities are not equally accessible, and economic hardship is experienced disproportionately by Chicago’s Black and Latinx residents, who largely reside in the city’s South, Southwest, West, and Northwest Sides.

To help aid inequality in the education sector, we have created this website to better help students from different districts collaborate. This collaboration will be based on academic and extra-curricular interests. We hope that this tool will help students have more access to the same resources.
